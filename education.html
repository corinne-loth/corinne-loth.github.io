Education

For the entirety of elementary, middle and high school education, Corinne remained in Pewaukee School District in Pewaukee, WI. It is home to over 2,500 students. As a student, Corinne was interested in english and history courses primarily and did not enjoy any math or science. From this tendency to favor writing and reading intensive courses, she knew she wanted to enter into a career with opportunties for growth in that area.

At Pewaukee High School, Corinne received a number of accolades to represent her academic achievements. Corinne was on the High Honor Roll each semester and enrolled in multiple Advanced Placement courses. At the end of her four years, Corinne had a GPA of 4.0 unweighted and 4.125 weighted, never receiving less than an A in any class. Furthermore, she was named English Student of the Year district-wide from 2020-2021.

Since enrolling at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, Corinne has continued to prioritize academics. Her first semester, she enrolled in a First Year Interest Group (FIG) specific to Internet Studies in order to continue her exploration of that niche and learn in a similar-minded group environment. Each semester Corinne has been on the Dean's List and currently has a cumulative GPA of 3.856, with high hopes for the future.

Notable courses Corinne has taken thus far include LIS301: Information Literacies in Online Spaces, LIS202: Informational Divides and Differences in a Multicultural Society, COM ARTS346: Critical Internet Studies, COM ARTS260: Communication and Human Behavior, COM ARTS361: Introduction to Quantitative Research in Communication, COM ARTS355: Introduction to Media Production, J201: Introduction to Mass Communication and both Italian 101 and Italian 102.
